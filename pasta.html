Lasagna Pasta

Italian dish made of stacked layers of this flat pasta alternating with fillings like vegetables, cheese, seasonings and spices such as garlic, oregano and basil.

Ravioli Pasta

Ravioli is a type of Italian food pasta dish. It has two layers of pasta dough with a filling between the two layers.

Tortellini Pasta Salad

Tortelloni are stuffed pasta, stuffed with ricotta cheese and leaf vegetables, such as parsley and/or spinach

Rigatoni con la Pajata

Pajata is the term for the intestines of an unweaned calf, cooked with a sort of thick, creamy, cheese-like sauce.

Trofie al pesto

Refer to the original dish pesto alla genovese & consists of crushed garlic, European pine nuts, coarse salt, basil leaves, hard cheese all blended with olive oil.

Carbonara Pasta

Carbonara is an Italian pasta dish from Rome made with egg, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper.

Ciceri e tria

Ciceri e tria is a pasta dish originated in Apulia,served as a primi piatti dish, a first course that consists of a pasta dish.

Arrabbiata Pasta

Arrabbiata sauce is a spicy sauce for pasta made from garlic, tomatoes, and dried red chili peppers cooked in olive oil.

Fettuccine Alfredo

Fettuccine Alfredo or fettuccine al burro is an Italian pasta dish of fresh fettuccine tossed with butter and Parmesan cheese
